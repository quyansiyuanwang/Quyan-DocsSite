# Legal policy management

Use this page to maintain legal-policy versions such as terms and privacy notices.

## Page purpose

- Maintain terms of service and privacy policy separately.
- Create, edit, publish, and unpublish versions.
- Control what legal text users see during registration or use.

## What you will see

### Type switch

- Terms of service.
- Privacy policy.

### Version list

- Version records.
- Published/current markers.
- Visible current version status.

### Management actions

- Create version.
- Edit version.
- Publish or unpublish.
- Delete version.

## Common actions

1. Switch to the policy type you need to maintain.
2. Create or edit the target version.
3. Preview the Markdown formatting.
4. Publish the new version when it is ready for users.

## Notes

- Registration, login, or verification flows can reference the published policy text from this page.
- Confirm whether older versions still need to stay available before removing them.
- This content usually requires coordination with legal or operations teams.
